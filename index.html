Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about rollout and access.

How do I get access?

Ask your team admin to grant you a role. Least-privilege defaults are enforced to ensure security while maintaining operational efficiency.

Can I roll back a feature?

Yes, feature rollbacks are instant and safe. Simply disable the flag or set exposure to 0%—changes apply immediately without any downtime.

Do you support audit exports?

Absolutely. CSV exports are available with comprehensive filters for user activity, event types, and custom time ranges to meet all compliance requirements.

How does the approval workflow work?

Changes require approval from authorized team members. You'll receive notifications for pending reviews and can track the status of all your requests in real-time.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required, and you can upgrade at any time during or after the trial period.
